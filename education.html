Jose Rizal's Education

Early Education (1870-1876)

Jose Rizal's early education was at a private school in Binan, Laguna, under the guidance of Maestro Justiniano Aquino Cruz. He showed exceptional intelligence and was sent to Manila for further studies.

Ateneo Municipal de Manila (1877-1882)

Rizal attended the Ateneo Municipal de Manila, earning a Bachelor of Arts degree with highest honors. He continued his studies there, focusing on philosophy and letters.

University of Santo Tomas (1882-1884)

He studied medicine at the University of Santo Tomas but was dissatisfied with the discrimination he faced. He decided to pursue his education abroad.

Universidad Central de Madrid (1884-1885)

Rizal traveled to Spain and enrolled at the Universidad Central de Madrid, where he completed his studies in medicine and philosophy. He earned his Licentiate in Medicine in 1885.

Further Studies and Travels (1885-1892)

After Madrid, Rizal continued his education in Paris and Heidelberg, specializing in ophthalmology. He traveled extensively across Europe, enhancing his knowledge and gaining exposure to various cultures and political ideas.
